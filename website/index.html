About Us

We’re a military simulation community aimed to emulate the real 30th Infantry Regiment through Bohemia’s Interactive latest series ArmA III. Our goal is to provide a “Serious but fun” environment by using real US Army tactics and doctrines. Our community consist of like-minded individuals who wish to achieve this goal and those who enjoy playing ArmA III in this manner.
